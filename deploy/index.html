The Seven Pillars

A journey from awakening to love

01

Repent

Change begins with the self. A turning, not guilt. A human being can change.

02

Presence

Now is the only place anything ever happens. Not the past. Not the future.

03

Stillness

In the quiet, you meet something steadier than mood or title — simply, "I am."

04

Awakening

What we seek is not outside us. It is dormant within, waiting to be awakened.

05

Know Yourself

See clearly — not who you perform as, but the raw, honest self without flinching.

06

Fill Your Own Cup

The cup was never empty. The work was never addition. It was recognition.

07

Love

A full cup exists to pour. To see no distance between self and other — that is love.

"You cannot pour from an empty cup —
but yours was never empty to begin with."
— Mindset.i Philosophy
